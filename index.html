South of the River

Quality STEM shouldn't require a 45-minute drive north

The corridor from Kwinana to Mandurah is home to thousands of families — and almost zero quality holiday coding programmes. Base Camp Code was built to fix that.

We run small, teacher-led camps where students actually build things: robots that avoid obstacles, games played between two BBC micro:bits, autonomous navigation challenges. Real projects with real outcomes.

Kwinana and Rockingham to start, expanding south toward Mandurah as we grow.

Years 5–9 welcome — complete beginners to confident block-coders, all catered for.

July school holidays 2026 — two identical cohorts, Week 1 and Week 2.

What We Stand For

Our values

Everything we do — the programme design, the pricing, the way we talk to parents — is rooted in five values.

📍
Accessible

Quality STEM education shouldn't require a drive north. We bring it to the communities that need it most.

🔧
Hands-on

Students build, break, debug and rebuild. Learning happens through doing — not watching someone else do it.

📈
Progressive

Every camp is a stepping stone. There is always a next level — in our programme, and in life.

👩‍🏫
Teacher-led

Real curriculum expertise, not a script. Every session is designed and delivered by someone who understands how young people learn.

🎒
Ownership

Students leave with something real — a device, a skill, and a sense of what they are genuinely capable of building.

What's Included

Everything your child needs — including the robot

💻

All equipment provided

Laptops, cables, track materials and all consumables. Just bring yourself.

🏆

Certificate of completion

Presented at the Day 3 showcase — a real moment of celebration in front of family.

👨‍👩‍👧

Parent showcase on Day 3

Parents are invited in for the Grand Prix finale — your child explains and demonstrates their robot live.

🤖

They keep the BBC micro:bit

Every student leaves camp with their own BBC micro:bit V2 to take home. It's theirs to keep — to experiment, to build, to show off. This is the single biggest differentiator from every other camp out there. The learning doesn't stop when camp does.
